About This Directory

This directory was curated in April 2026 to help researchers, students, and practitioners find open-access Indonesian journals that publish Internet of Things (IoT) research. All listed journals are freely accessible online and have published IoT-related content from 2020 onward.

Journals are indexed in SINTA (Science and Technology Index — Indonesia's national journal ranking system) and/or Scopus. Accreditation levels range from SINTA 2 (highest national) to SINTA 4.
